Our Story

Crafted in the Heart of Australia

PeterMat was founded with a simple belief: Australian athletes deserve equipment that matches their spirit—tough, resilient, and built for the unique conditions of our land.

From the sun-scorched outback to the coastal breaks, our gear is tested in Australia's most demanding environments. Every stitch, every material, every design choice reflects our commitment to authentic Australian craftsmanship.
